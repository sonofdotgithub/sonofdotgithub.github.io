About

Son Of Dot is a community-driven token that evolutionize the yield-generating contract on the Binance Smart Chain: you get rewarded in DOT instead of tokens. How does it work? It works by using a proxy contract that distributes the Polkadot rewards. You will get airdropped your rewards four times a day without the need to claim them somewhere. Just sit back and watch your DOT bags grow.

Features

2% DOT Reflections 2% of every transaction is taken and redistributed to all $SOD holders. Hold $SOD tokens, earn DOT.
9% Marketing Fee 9% of every transaction is taken and will be used for enhancements, operations, and marketing of the project.
1% Liquidity Pool 1% of every transaction is transformed into liquidity. It's automatic and helps create a price floor.
Manual Claim Rewards You can claim your earned DOT by using a proxy contract that distributes the Polkadot rewards.
1% Max Transaction Sells are restricted to less than 1% of the total supply to reduce swing-trading and break whales' control.
Secured By Design Initial Liquidity Provided will be locked with PinkSale. This ensures that the project will not be rug-pulled.
